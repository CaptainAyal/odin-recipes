Oooey Gooey Lasagna

Here you'll find a real recipe for lasagna that I made last week. Quite simple recipe actually. Only requires a few special tools such as a blowtorch and strawberry de-seeder After 3-8 hours of work you'll have this delectable cheesey cake...i mean lasagna. It's got layers so it's the same.

Ingredients

  • Cheese...duh
  • Flour
  • Red sauce
  • Butter maybe
  • and some spices. Anything that's been sitting in the back of the cabinet will do

Recipe

  1. Great the cheez
  2. Cream the cheese with the flour set aside
  3. De-seed your berries. Yep, you gotta get them all. Use a de-seeder. Should take you about 10 minutes per berry, so like 2 hours
  4. Mash berries into sauce, use some butter if it helps
  5. Don't forget to add spices at any point
  6. Pour chez mizzture over the mess
  7. Blow torch until done
  8. Set on dog food dish to cool
  9. Serve with your favorite Matza Ball Towers
